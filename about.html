About Arc Technical Services

Our legacy of honesty, integrity, and efficiency.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to meet the global and ever-expanding demand for efficient distribution of CAPEX and MRO across all industrial sectors. To be the premier choice of industries that want to outsource their procurement function.

We aim to achieve our mission through:

  • Providing a premier level of service to our customers worldwide
  • Leveraging our expanding global network of manufacturer and supplier agreements
  • Offering time‑critical and flexible logistical solutions
  • Implementing operational cost efficiencies to pass on competitive pricing to our clients

Our Reputation

With a family legacy that began in the 1970s by Mr. Hiranand Mahtani, we represent the 3rd generation within the business sector and are proud of our long‑standing reputation for honesty and integrity.

Our core principle is to provide as much transparency as possible to the supply chain, which has rewarded us with a loyal customer base. At times, solving challenges requires a level of flexibility and support which can only be achieved through long‑term relationships based on trust.

“Having operated Arc Technical Services Ltd (formerly Gibson Exports Ltd) for more than half of its lifespan, I am proud of the reputation for honesty and efficiency that our company has earned by always putting its clients’ needs first. We look forward to adding value to your organisation.”

— Yogesh M. Daryanani, Director

Our Team

Our staff is composed of qualified, multi‑disciplined engineers, accountants, administrators, and experienced logistics professionals with decades of experience in the field of international procurement.

We work together to meet our targets for a high standard of integrity, customer service, and cost efficiency, while thriving on the success of overcoming complex procurement challenges faced by our clients. We consider our staff as part of the family and maintain good team morale and a great working atmosphere.
